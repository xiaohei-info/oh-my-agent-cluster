# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, safe, and productive community for everyone,
regardless of experience, identity, background, or ability.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and evidence, not people.
- Make room for newcomers and different levels of technical experience.
- Protect private information and remove secrets or personal data from shared
  logs, screenshots, examples, and reports.
- Accept maintainer decisions about scope while remaining free to disagree
  respectfully.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, deliberate
intimidation, sustained disruption, doxxing, or publishing another person's
private information without permission are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove content, close or lock threads, reject changes,
or temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior harms the
community. Enforcement decisions will consider severity, context, repetition,
and impact.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through the contact
options on the [xiaohei-info GitHub profile](https://github.com/xiaohei-info).
Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical. Retaliation against a
reporter is prohibited.

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces and when someone officially
represents the project in another community.
